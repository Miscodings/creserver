Glyphs

Glyphs are also probably a big reason behind you choosing to have a resource pack on your server. They allow you to have cool rank icons, rank tags, or even display your server's logo anywhere on your server, whether it be your TAB, your Scoreboard, or even on a Hologram!

Glyph Config → Texture

Config (.yml)

→

Texture (.png)

Here's An Example:

Nexo/items/skill_icons.yml

server_logo:
  texture: logo/server_logo
  height: 50
  ascent: 40
  char: ꑂ

- `server_logo` is the ID of the glyph. Just like how every item has its own ID, so does every glyph.

- The texture (or picture), that the glyph is currently using is in the provided path.

- Just like how items have custom-model-data, glyphs have unicodes! And just like with custom-model-data, if you do not specify any, Nexo will generate unicodes for your glyphs when you reload the plugin.

Heights & Ascents

Glyphs have `height` and `ascent` properties (these are likely renamed on different resource pack management plugins).

The `height` of a glyph is almost like its scale. `height` can NEVER be lower than `ascent`.

The `ascent` of a glyph is its vertical offset. `ascent` can NEVER be higher than `height`.

Once your configuration is complete, copy-paste that glyph literally anywhere in-game, whether its in chat as an emoji or on a hologram as your logo, and watch your image show up!

Custom GUIs:

Using this concept, you can create custom GUIs using glyphs, and simply copy-paste the unicode corresponding to the glyph in the menu's title!
